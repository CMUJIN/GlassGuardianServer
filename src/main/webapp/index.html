How Glass Guardian Help You When You Are In Danger

1) Sign in with your gmail account which binds with your Google Glass


2) Set up alert email list, so your friends and family know what happen to you in the first time if you were in danger


3) After install the glassware, turn on the Guardian glassware when you think you are in unsafe area with potential dangers


4) When you face the danger and trigger alert, an alert notification email will be sent immediately to all the email address on your list along with a tracking data link


5) Your friends/family open the tracking link, they will see all the tracking information of you and know what exactly happened to you
